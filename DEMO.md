# BASTION — 90-second demo script

**URL:** https://bastion-xi.vercel.app · zoom ≥125% · dark room.

## Open with the insight (15s, before you click)
> "Brands are losing the prompts that drive revenue *inside AI answers* — and they find out weeks later, if ever. Profound shows you the gap. **Bastion is the agent that closes it — it writes, distributes, and publishes the content to win the citation back, at the scale of 100 marketers.**"

## DIAGNOSE tab (15s)
> "This is Anthropic, real Profound data. **23% Share of Voice, rank #4** — Grok, xAI and Gemini are ahead. Here are the exact prompts we're losing."
- **Click any gap row** → "The agent tears down *why* the competitor wins, and *exactly what our page must cover* to take it back."

## EXECUTE tab — the agent works (45s)
> "Now it executes."
1. **The ad budget slider:** "I set how much to spend on ads per day — ads buy the #1 spot *now* while our free content climbs."
2. **▶ Execute** → "Watch it write **full, publish-ready pages live** — and a **LinkedIn post, X thread, Reddit answer, and outreach email** for each. That's the whole distribution, done."
3. **Click a channel tab** (LinkedIn / X) → "Every channel, ready to ship."
4. **Click "Publish"** → a **real live page opens** (`/p/...`, schema-marked). "It didn't just write it — it *shipped* it."
5. Point to **Ads-now-content-later**: "And it turns the ads **off** automatically once our content ranks — you stop paying once you own the answer for free."
6. **Start Autopilot** → "Left alone, it just keeps going — generating and publishing, continuously. One person, the work of a hundred."

## Close (15s)
> "Real Profound data in. Real content, real pages, real campaign, a CMO-ready brief out. Point it at any brand. **This is the marketing engineer as one person running a hundred-person content operation.**" → **Open brief** (the Profound-branded deliverable).

## If anything stalls
The baked-in real content keeps the demo running with no network. Re-load the tab. The Vercel URL is the backup.
