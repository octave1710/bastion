# BASTION — live demo script (≤ 4 min, target 90s core)

> **Golden rule:** lead with the INSIGHT, not the tool. Marketing Insight (30%) +
> Quantifiable Impact (25%) = 55% of the score, and both are won in the framing.

## Controls
- **`Space`** or **`▶ Run Demo`** — fire the whole sequence from one trigger.
- **`R`** / **`Esc`** / **Reset** — back to peacetime. (`Exit ⎋` on the scale screen.)
- Zoom the browser to ≥125%. Full-screen the window. Dark room.

## The cold open (say this BEFORE you touch anything) — ~15s
> "Brands are losing the prompts that drive revenue inside AI answers — and they
> find out weeks later, if ever. Bastion treats answer-share as a **dollar
> portfolio** and defends it in real time, arbitraging **paid vs organic**.
> Here's the war room."

## Then press `Space`. Narrate over the beats:

**(0–10s) Opening — peacetime.**
> "Persona: growth lead at Anthropic. Defended Revenue: **$4.2M a month**. The grid
> is every high-intent prompt, green where we win the AI citation. Top-right: the
> exact dollar model — volume × share × click-through × conversion × ACV. It's a
> model, and we show it."

**(10–25s) The attack.**
> "OpenAI just overtook us on **'best AI for coding'** — worth **$1.2M a year**.
> The cell flips red. Revenue at Risk spikes. No human has noticed yet. The agent has."

**(25–65s) The riposte — let the reasoning stream.**
> "Watch it think. It **tears down** the competitor's new benchmark page and pulls
> the exact 3 claims engines are quoting. It **values** the position — $1.2M, defend.
> Then it does what a team of 100 can't: it **allocates the whole 2,400-prompt
> portfolio by ROI** — defends the valuable ones, and **skips** the ones that cost
> more than they're worth. Judgment, not blind action."
>
> "The core insight: **paid is a temporary bridge, organic is the permanent win.**
> $2,700 of paid holds the position for ~8 days while the organic counter-page ranks
> — then paid tapers to zero. It drafts that page, and **grades its own work**: first
> draft 6.8, below the bar — it **revises** — 8.4, beats them 3/3, ship. Bid rec,
> counter-page, Slack alert. It **acts.**"

**(65–85s) The payoff.**
> "Share recovers. The cell goes green. Revenue at Risk back to **zero**. Defended
> Revenue ticks up. The position is reconquered."

**(85–100s) The scale reveal.**
> "And it does this **everywhere, right now, while I talk** — 2,400 positions,
> $50M/yr, defended and triaged by ROI continuously. One person, the work of a
> hundred. **Point it at any brand — plug-and-play.**"

## Close (one line)
> "Same system, two faces: this war room, and a **Profound-native agent** that
> actually runs the loop on your data. You could turn it on today."

## If the live demo breaks
- Hit **`R`** and re-run — it's deterministic, it will replay identically.
- If the machine is wedged, switch to the **backup video** (record it during the
  locked window). Narrate over it, never apologize, keep moving.

## Backup video recording checklist
1. Browser at ≥125%, full screen, dark theme, window 1440×900+.
2. Screen-record. Press `Space`. Let the full sequence play to the scale reveal.
3. Re-run once; keep the cleanest take. Keep it under 2 minutes.
